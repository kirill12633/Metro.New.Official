About Our Project

Metro New is a large-scale Roblox project dedicated to the Moscow Metro. We lovingly recreate stations, tunnels, and trains to give you the most realistic underground experience possible.

Our project is constantly evolving - we add new stations, improve graphics, and expand gameplay features. Join our growing community!

Game Features

Realistic Trains

Control various Moscow Metro train models with detailed interiors and controls

Accurate Stations

Explore stations recreated with attention to architectural features and details

Multiplayer Mode

Play with friends, drive trains together, and create your own routes

Development & Updates

We constantly add new content, improve gameplay, and expand the metro map
